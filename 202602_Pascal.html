CIRE Faculty Spotlight: Jennifer Pascal#

20251203 CIRE Spotlight: Jennifer Pascal

Professor Jennifer Pascal went to TN Tech for her B.S. and Ph.D., both in Chemical Engineering. She participated in the NIH Academic Science and Research Training program as a postdoctoral fellow at the University of New Mexico, where Prof. Pascal did cancer research and taught at a tribal college. Prof. Pascal started a chemical engineering brewery engineering class in 2019, and UConn Brewing Innovation began in 2023 in collaboration with Jen Mathieu (CCEI) and Pete Menard. Since then, they have grown it to add more courses and experiential learning opportunities for students.
